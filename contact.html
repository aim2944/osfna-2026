Frequently Asked Questions

When does registration open?

Team registration opens June 1, 2026. Early registration (before May 31) receives a $50 discount. Late registration July 11–20 carries a $50 surcharge.

Where is OSFNA 2026 being held?

The 2026 venue will be announced in early 2026. Past events have been held in cities including Seattle, Minneapolis, and Dallas. Follow our social media for the announcement.

How many players can be on a roster?

Soccer teams may carry up to 22 players (minimum 12). Basketball rosters allow up to 12 players (minimum 8). Golf teams are 4 players.

Can I vendor or sponsor OSFNA 2026?

Yes! Vendor booth applications open April 2026. Sponsorship packages at Gold, Silver, and Community Partner levels are available. Email myosfna@gmail.com to discuss.

Is OSFNA open to non-Oromo players?

All athletes are welcome to compete regardless of background. Each team must have at least 50% Oromo players, consistent with OSFNA bylaws.

Vendors at the Night Events

This vendor program is for the 7 official Oromo Week night events only — not the daytime soccer tournament. We're placing food, hookah, lounge, and merch operators at the venues where the party is happening, in the windows where people actually spend: pre-show lobby, between sets, and the late-night food rush after doors close.

Vendor Slots

Lobby Table
Merch, apparel, jewelry, accessories. Posted in the pre-show lobby and active during artist sets.
  • Single or multi-night slots
  • High visibility window: doors-open through last call
  • Power access included
Late-Night Food
Food truck or kitchen pop-up posted outside the venue. Hungry crowd at 11pm–2am — best per-customer spend window of the week.
  • Outdoor / curbside placement
  • Multi-night slots available
  • Permits coordinated with venue
Hookah · Lounge
Hookah operators and premium lounge partners. Best fit for All-Star Night, Championship Eve, and the Closing Gala.
  • Designated lounge zone
  • Featured in GC announcements
  • Priority on upgrade nights

Pricing is per-night and depends on placement and category. Reach out below for a quote — we'll match you to the right nights and venue zone.

The OSFNA marketplace is one of the highest-traffic Oromo community events in North America. Reach thousands of engaged diaspora customers in a single week.

Join OSFNA's Marketplace

OSFNA brings together thousands of community members to celebrate Oromo culture and sport. Our marketplace is the vibrant heart of Oromo Week — a place where local entrepreneurs, food vendors, and service providers connect with customers. Whether you offer food, merchandise, services, or cultural products, OSFNA's vendor marketplace is the perfect platform to reach the Oromo diaspora across North America.

Oromo Week 2026

Culture · Sport · Community Celebration

Beyond the pitch, Oromo Week is a full week of celebration — nightlife, daytime activities, cultural showcases, and community gatherings organized by OSFNA, public entities, and private enterprises. This is where the Oromo diaspora comes home.

👑
Miss Oromo North America
Running since 2003, this beloved annual program celebrates Oromo women and culture. MONA 2025 crowned Ofifan Dabala.
🎵
Nightly Concerts
Star-studded concerts featuring renowned Oromo artists every night. Past performers include Gutu Abera, Kemer Yusuf, and Wako Getacho.
🎭
Cultural Performances
Traditional Oromo dance, music, and cultural exhibitions performed before matches and during halftime.
☀️
Daytime Activities
Community forums, youth programming, exhibitions, and family-friendly events for all ages.
🌐
Community Forums
Panel discussions, heritage exhibitions, and organizing sessions connecting Oromos around shared goals.
🛍️
Vendors & Marketplace
OSFNA collaborates with local businesses offering merchandise, food, and services during the festival.
